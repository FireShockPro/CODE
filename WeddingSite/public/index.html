Our Story

It all started with a swipe right in 2020. What began as a simple Tinder match quickly turned into something truly special. From our very first date at Lone Star — my favorite restaurant — we both just knew. Conversation flowed effortlessly, laughter came easily, and by the end of the night, it felt like we’d known each other forever.

Over the next six months, we built a foundation of love, trust, and friendship. We spent countless hours talking, laughing, and dreaming about the future. It wasn’t long before we decided to take the next step — moving in together and adding another furry friend to our little family.

Six months later, we packed up our lives and moved to London, Ontario, ready to start a new chapter. For the past five years, we’ve been building our home, our careers, and our life together. Through every challenge and every joy, our love has only grown stronger.

Then, on our 4th anniversary, he got down on one knee and asked me to spend forever with him. It was the easiest “yes” of my life.

Now, as we get ready to say “I do,” we’re so excited to celebrate this next chapter surrounded by the people who have supported and loved us along the way.
