Frequently Asked Questions

What time should I arrive?

Please arrive at the reception by 1:30pm. We have a shuttle bus that will take you to the ceremony space.

What is the dress code?

The dress code is whatever you are comfortable with. Please wear comfortable shoes as we'll have plenty of dancing! Please no Burgundy or White.

Can I bring a guest?

We have a limited amount of space at our venues. We had to unfortunately limit the amount of guests we invite. Sorry for any inconveniences. If you have questions, please reach out to us directly.

Are there dietary restrictions accommodated?

Yes! Please let us know about any dietary restrictions when you RSVP, and we'll make sure to accommodate them.

Where can I park?

Parking will be available at 3500 Fallowfield Road.

Accommodations

Please select the accommodations page for all information on hotels.

What if I need to cancel my RSVP?

Please reach out to us as soon as possible at wedding@gouthro.net.

Can I take photos or videos?

Absolutely! We'd love to see your perspective on the day. Feel free to capture moments, but please be respectful during the ceremony and let the photographer do their work during formal photos.

Will there be food and drinks?

Yes! We'll have a delicious reception with food and beverages to celebrate with us. If you have any dietary restrictions, please let us know on your RSVP form.

What is the RSVP deadline?

Please RSVP by August 1st, 2026. This helps us plan the right amount of food and seating. If you need more time, contact us directly.

Is there a wedding gift registry?

Your presence is the greatest gift! If you'd like to give a gift, we have registries set up. Please ask Kelby or Andrew for details.

Can I bring my children?

Children are welcome! Please let us know their ages when you RSVP so we can plan accordingly. We'll have activities to keep them entertained.

What should I do if I have a plus-one not listed on my invitation?

Please contact us as soon as possible at wedding@gouthro.net. Due to space limitations, we may not be able to accommodate additional guests, but we'll do our best to work with you.

Will transportation be provided?

Yes! We have a shuttle bus departing at 1:30 PM from Legion 641 that will take guests to the ceremony. Parking is available at 3500 Fallowfield Road.
