Frequently Asked Questions

What time should I arrive?

Please arrive at the reception location at 3500 Fallowfield Road by 1:30pm. We have a shuttle bus that will take you to the ceremony space.

What is the dress code?

The dress code is semi-formal. The ceremony is outdoors, the reception is indoors. Please wear comfortable shoes as we'll have plenty of dancing! Please no Burgundy or White.

Can I bring a guest?

We have a limited amount of space at our venues. We had to unfortunately limit the amount of guests we invite. We apologize for any inconvenience. If you have questions, please reach out to us directly or email us at wedding@gouthro.net.

Are there dietary restrictions accommodated?

Yes! Please let us know about any dietary restrictions when you RSVP, and we'll make sure to accommodate them.

Is there parking available?

There will be free parking available at the reception venue at 3500 Fallowfield Road and a shuttle will be provided to and from the ceremony location.

Are there hotel accommodations available?

Please visit the accommodations page for all information on pre-booked hotel blocks.

What if I need to cancel my RSVP?

Please reach out to us as soon as possible directly or via email at wedding@gouthro.net.

Can I take photos or videos?

For our ceremony, we're asking guests to keep it unplugged and photo free so we can all be present in the moment. That said, we absolutely encourage everyone to take photos and videos during the reception and capture all the fun as the night unfolds.

Will there be food and drinks?

Yes! We'll have a delicious dinner at the reception with a bar available for beverages. Please note that it is a CASH bar. They do not accept debit or credit cards, but there is an ATM available. If you have any dietary restrictions, please let us know on your RSVP form.

What is the RSVP deadline? How do we RSVP?

Please RSVP by August 1st, 2026. We have an online RSVP on this website, but we will also be accepting mail in and text RSVP's. This helps us plan the right amount of food and seating. If you need more time, contact us directly or via email at wedding@gouthro.net.

Is there a wedding gift registry?

Your presence is the greatest gift! If you'd like to give a gift, we will have a card box available at the reception and would prefer cash gifts to go towards our honeymoon fund. Please ask Kelby or Andrew for details.

Will transportation be provided?

Yes! We have a shuttle bus departing at 1:30 PM from Legion 641 that will take guests to the ceremony. Parking is available at 3500 Fallowfield Road.

Can I post photos on social media?

Yes, feel free to post photos and please tag us and use our hashtag: #kelbrew — we’d love to see your pics!

How can we reach out to the bride and groom?

You can reach out to us directly at wedding@gouthro.net. We're happy to answer any questions or provide additional information.
