Frequently Asked Questions

What time should I arrive?

Please arrive at the reception by 1:30pm. We have a shuttle bus that will take you to the ceremony space. 3500 Fallowfield Road.

What is the dress code?

The dress code is whatever you are comfortable with. The ceremony is outdoors, the reception is indoors. Please wear comfortable shoes as we'll have plenty of dancing! Please no Burgundy or White.

Can I bring a guest?

We have a limited amount of space at our venues. We had to unfortunately limit the amount of guests we invite. Sorry for any inconveniences. If you have questions, please reach out to us directly.

Are there dietary restrictions accommodated?

Yes! Please let us know about any dietary restrictions when you RSVP, and we'll make sure to accommodate them.

Where can I park?

Parking will be available at 3500 Fallowfield Road. It is free parking.

Accommodations

Please select the accommodations page for all information on hotels.

What if I need to cancel my RSVP?

Please reach out to us as soon as possible at wedding@gouthro.net.

Can I take photos or videos?

For our ceremony, we're asking guests to keep it unplugged and photo free so we can all be present in the moment. That said, we absolutely encourage everyone to take photos and videos during the reception and capture all the fun as the night unfolds.

Will there be food and drinks?

Yes! We'll have a delicious reception with food and beverages to celebrate with us. Please note that it is a CASH bar. They do not accept debit or credit cards. If you have any dietary restrictions, please let us know on your RSVP form.

What is the RSVP deadline? How do we RSVP?

Please RSVP by August 1st, 2026. We will have an online RSVP but we will also be accepting mail in and text RSVP's. This helps us plan the right amount of food and seating. If you need more time, contact us directly.

Is there a wedding gift registry?

Your presence is the greatest gift! If you'd like to give a gift, we will have a card box available at the reception. Please ask Kelby or Andrew for details.

Will transportation be provided?

Yes! We have a shuttle bus departing at 1:30 PM from Legion 641 that will take guests to the ceremony. Parking is available at 3500 Fallowfield Road.

Can I post photos on social media

Yes, please tag us and use our hashtag: #kelbrew —we’d love to see your pics!

How can we reach out to the bride and groom?

You can reach out to us directly at wedding@gouthro.net. We're happy to answer any questions or provide additional information.
